'Children in Art'

Research

My interest in children -> ballet
the way the react to each other 

At the beginning of my research I worked with a doll. My first purpose was the find the correct way to portray a childlike figure. That's why I used my doll. I would put her down underneath a light and study her. I made a few sketches and paintings to controle the right technique. The next step was making the colors and shape work on my paintings. I had to find a way to combine my doll or child with the background. To do this, I took a colorfull carpet with geometric shapes. I made these shapes my own and made a whole other image by constantly changing the perspectif. 

Later I took photographs of children running around and playing in an unfamiliar inverionnement. I wanted to see how they reacted to there new surroundings. After a while they forget I was there following them around with a camera and started to talk freely to each other. It was a strange experience to see how a childs mind really works.
